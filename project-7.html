Case Study

The Digital Stage

Summary

In close partnership with the Executive Producer of Brand and the Vice President of Digital, I helped lead the creative and operational execution of the Kennedy Center’s The Digital Stage, shaping it into a flagship digital brand that expanded the institution’s reach through award-winning storytelling and large-scale audience growth.

Role • Creative Producer, Project Manager, Multimedia Lead Digital Brand • The Digital Stage — YouTube & Social Storytelling Impact • Grew the channel to over one million followers and delivered Webby- and Anthem-recognized content

Overview

The Kennedy Center’s The Digital Stage is the institution’s primary digital brand, extending its mission beyond the physical campus and bringing performances, artist stories, and original programming to global audiences. Working alongside the Executive Producer of Brand and the Vice President of Digital, I served as the lead multimedia producer responsible for translating strategy into execution.

Together, we launched and grew the The Digital Stage YouTube channel into a platform with over one million subscribers. I focused on production leadership, storytelling execution, team management, and delivery pipelines, ensuring that creative vision, brand identity, and technical production aligned. The channel became known for its range of content, from intimate artist profiles to large-scale performance moments featuring artists such as Cynthia Erivo, Ben Folds, Dave Chappelle, and many others.

The work received widespread recognition, including Webby and Anthem Awards, establishing The Digital Stage as a respected media brand in the performing arts space.

Details

  • Role: Creative Producer and Project Manager working in collaboration with the Executive Producer of Brand and the Vice President of Digital to execute the vision, strategy, and growth of the The Digital Stage platform.
  • Scope:
    • Launched and scaled the The Digital Stage YouTube channel to over one million followers
    • Led multimedia production execution while aligning with brand and digital leadership
    • Managed production workflows, schedules, and delivery pipelines
    • Oversaw storytelling pieces, artist features, and original digital series
    • Partnered with marketing and distribution teams to maximize reach and engagement
  • Team:
    • Executive Producer of Brand
    • Vice President of Digital
    • In-house producers, editors, animators, and social media teams
    • Artists, performers, and production partners
  • Impact:
    • Helped establish The Digital Stage as the Kennedy Center’s flagship digital brand
    • Expanded access to world-class performing arts content for global audiences
